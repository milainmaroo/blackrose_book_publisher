Tess Gerritsen

Tess Gerritsen is a mystery and thrillers author and her first medical thriller novel, Harvest was marked on number thirteen at New York Times bestseller lists. Although most of her recent books have been in the Rizzoli/Isles series, in 2007 Gerritsen wrote a stand-alone historical thriller titled The Bone Garden. Gerritsen's books have been published in 40 countries and have sold 25 million copies. Read More
