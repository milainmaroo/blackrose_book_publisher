Lee Child

Lee Child focuses on thriller novels and is famous for his Jack Reacher novel series. He has won the Anthony Award and the Barry Award for his novel, Killing Floor. Some books in the Reacher series are written in the first person, while others are written in the third person. Lee Child has characterised the books as revenge stories – "Somebody does a very bad thing, and Reacher takes revenge" – driven by his anger at the downsizing at Granada. Although English, he deliberately chose to write American-style thrillers. Read More
