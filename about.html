About Our Organization

Our "Black Rose Publishing" house is first established in Spring Hill, Brisbane in the year 1995. After three years in 1998, we expanded our business to Sydney and Melbourne in 2001. We have over 15 years of reputation and we are also aiming to grow our business other areas in Australia. Our dedication is to support young authors, bring international bestselling authors together and become one of the best publishers in crime fiction genre.

We are connecting with many bestselling authors and supporting amateur authors to reach their full potential in writing noir crime or detective fictions. We do believe that noir fiction is an art and we are publishing authentic and fascinating work of arts from our authors worldwide. We have over 50 million fictional readers and customers around the world and we are always trying our best to offer the incredible and memorable services to our customers. Moreover, we are helping young writers to develop their skillsets in writing noir crime fictions and we have a welcoming and networking event in Sydney, Melbourne and Brisbane every month in order to to meet our amateur and professional authors as well as our partners and share our company's values and dedications on noir crime fiction.
